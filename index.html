A bit about me

I'm a Vancouver-based UX/UI designer with a background in frontend development and a genuine passion for architecture — sketchbook always close by.

I explored architecture for a while although never finished the degree, but it left me with a deep love for sketching buildings, thinking in systems, and caring obsessively about spatial logic. That passion bleeds into everything I design.

My work tends to live at the intersection of research, craft, and technical feasibility. I can actually build the things I design. No pixel left unjustified.
